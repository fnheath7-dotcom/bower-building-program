6-Week Strength & Hypertrophy Program

DAY 1 – BENCH HEAVY

Bench: 5x3 @ RPE 7
Incline DB: 3–4x10–12
Cable Fly: 3x12–15
Triceps Pushdown: 3–4x12–15

DAY 2 – SQUAT

Squat: 5x5 @ RPE 7
Hack Squat: 3x10
Leg Press: 3x12
Leg Extension: 3x15

DAY 3 – BACK + SHOULDERS

Lat Pulldown: 4x10–12
Chest-supported row: 3x10
Cable row: 3x12
Lateral raise: 3x15
Rear delt fly: 3x15
Face pull: 3x15

DAY 4 – DEADLIFT + HAMSTRINGS

Deadlift: 3x3 @ 70–72%
RDL: 3x8
Ham curl: 3–4x12
Glute bridge: 3x10

DAY 5 – BENCH VOLUME + ARMS

Bench: 4x6 @ RPE 7
Incline DB: 3x10
Dips: 3x10
Curl: 3x12–15
Hammer curl: 3x12
Triceps ext: 3x12
🔼 WEEK 2 PROGRESSION
+2.5–5 lb compounds
Bench: 5x3 @ RPE 7.5
Squat: 5x5 → 4x5 @ RPE 7.5
Deadlift: 3x3 @ 72–75%
Accessories unchanged
🔵 WEEK 3 – INTENSIFICATION

BENCH:
1x1 @ RPE 8
3x3 @ 80%

SQUAT:
1x1 @ RPE 8
3x3 @ 80%

DEADLIFT:
3x2 @ 75–80% (NO singles)

Accessories: reduce only 1 set per exercise
🔵 WEEK 4 – OVERLOAD

BENCH:
1x1 @ RPE 8.5
3x2 @ 85%

SQUAT:
1x1 @ RPE 8–8.5
3x2 @ 82–85%

DEADLIFT:
2–3x2 @ 80–83%

Accessories: keep 70–80% volume, no failure sets
🔵 WEEK 5 – PEAK WEEK

Bench: single 90–97% + 2x2 @ 80%
Squat: single 88–95% + 2x2 @ 75–80%
Deadlift: optional single 85–92%

Recovery day: pump only (RPE 6–7)
Speed bench: 6x2 @ 65–70%
🧠 WEEK 6 – POST PEAK RESET

50–60% normal volume
RPE 6–7 max
NO singles
NO grinders
NO failure sets

Purpose: recovery + growth lock-in
