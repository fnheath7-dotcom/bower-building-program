6-Week Strength Program

MONDAY – BENCH HEAVY

Bench: 5x3 @ RPE 7
Incline DB: 3–4x10–12
Cable Fly: 3x12–15
Triceps Pushdown: 3–4x12–15

TUESDAY – SQUAT

Squat: 5x5 @ RPE 7
Hack Squat: 3x10
Leg Press: 3x12
Leg Extension: 3x15

WEDNESDAY – BACK + SHOULDERS

Lat Pulldown: 4x10–12
Chest-supported row: 3x10
Cable row: 3x12
Lateral raise: 3x15
Rear delt fly: 3x15
Face pull: 3x15

THURSDAY – DEADLIFT + HAMSTRINGS

Deadlift: 3x3 @ 70–72%
RDL: 3x8
Ham curl: 3–4x12
Glute bridge: 3x10

FRIDAY – BENCH VOLUME + ARMS

Bench: 4x6 @ RPE 7
Incline DB: 3x10
Dips: 3x10
Curl: 3x12–15
Hammer curl: 3x12
Triceps ext: 3x12
WEEK 1 – TOTAL SETS PER MUSCLE Chest: 21–22 sets Back: 10 sets Shoulders: 9 sets Triceps: 18–19 sets Biceps: 6 sets Quads: 14 sets Hamstrings/Glutes: 12–13 sets

WEEK 2 – BASE BUILD (4-DAY SPLIT)

Same structure as Week 1 with: - Bench: 5x3 @ RPE 7.5 - Squat: 5x5 → 4x5 @ RPE 7.5 - Deadlift: 3x3 @ 72–75% - +2.5–5 lb compounds
WEEK 2 – TOTAL SETS PER MUSCLE (SAME AS WEEK 1) Chest: 21–22 sets Back: 10 sets Shoulders: 9 sets Triceps: 18–19 sets Biceps: 6 sets Quads: 14 sets Hamstrings/Glutes: 12–13 sets
WEEK 3 – INTENSIFICATION Bench: 1x1 @ RPE 8 + 3x3 @ 80% Squat: 1x1 @ RPE 8 + 3x3 @ 80% Deadlift: 3x2 @ 75–80% (NO singles) Accessories: -1 set each exercise TOTAL SETS: Chest: 10–11 Back: 7 Shoulders: 6 Triceps: 10–11 Biceps: 4 Quads: 10 Hamstrings/Glutes: 9–10
WEEK 4 – OVERLOAD Bench: 1x1 @ RPE 8.5 + 3x2 @ 85% Squat: 1x1 @ 8–8.5 + 3x2 @ 82–85% Deadlift: 2–3x2 @ 80–83% Volume: 70–80% accessories, no failure TOTAL SETS (same as Week 3): Chest: 10–11 Back: 7 Shoulders: 6 Triceps: 10–11 Biceps: 4 Quads: 10 Hamstrings/Glutes: 9–10
WEEK 5 – PEAK WEEK Bench: 90–97% single + 2x2 @ 80% Squat: 88–95% single + 2x2 @ 75–80% Deadlift: optional 85–92% single Back: pump only (2–3 sets per movement) Speed bench: 6x2 @ 65–70% TOTAL SETS: Chest: 9–11 Back: 5–10 Shoulders: 4–9 Triceps: 6–10 Biceps: 0–3 Quads: 3–6 Hamstrings/Glutes: 1–5
WEEK 6 – POST PEAK RESET 50–60% volume RPE 6–7 max NO singles, NO grinders, NO failure sets TOTAL SETS: Chest: 11–13 Back: 5–6 Shoulders: 4–5 Triceps: 9–11 Biceps: 3 Quads: 7–8 Hamstrings/Glutes: 6–8
